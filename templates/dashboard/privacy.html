Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2024

At FrenchLMS, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or purchase a course. This may include:

  • Name and email address
  • Profile information (optional)
  • Payment details (processed securely via SSLCommerz)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our learning platform
  • Process your transactions and enrollments
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Communications about new courses and promotions (only if you opt-in)

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your sensitive data is encrypted and stored behind secured networks.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, understand site usage, and improve our content. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some platform features.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except for trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and processing payments.

Have questions about your data privacy? Contact our Privacy Team
